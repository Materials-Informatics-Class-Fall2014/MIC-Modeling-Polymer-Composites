--- layout: post title: Theoretical Models for Composite Material Response references: - http://www.scirp.org/journal/PaperInformation.aspx?PaperID=17712#.VGZv2vnF98E --- ### Voigt model The Voigt model is as simple relationship between the moduli of the components and the effective modulus of the composite. (Briefly described in Reference 1) E = E1*v1 +E2*v2 This is a simple mixing rule for an iso-strain state. This model assumes that the reinforcement is aligned in a single direction and that each component experiences the same strain.
